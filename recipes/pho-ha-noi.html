Pho Ha Noi - The taste of Hanoi

Description

Phở Hà Nội, or Hanoi-style beef noodle soup, is one of Vietnam’s most iconic dishes. It features flat rice noodles served in a fragrant, clear beef broth, topped with tender slices of beef and garnished with scallions and herbs. The flavor of the broth comes from slow-simmered beef bones and a blend of aromatic spices such as star anise, cinnamon, and ginger.

Unlike the southern variation, Phở Hà Nội is less sweet, more delicate in flavor, and served with minimal garnish—allowing the rich broth and quality of the beef to shine. It is enjoyed at any time of the day, but a steaming bowl of phở in the early morning is a cherished Hanoi tradition.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg beef bones (marrow and knuckle bones)
  • 500 g beef brisket
  • 300 g raw beef (eye round or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • 500 g flat rice noodles (bánh phở)
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 piece of ginger (about 8 cm), halved
  • 4–5 star anise pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4–5 cloves
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (or to taste)
  • Rock sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh scallions, cilantro, and lime wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Blanch beef bones and brisket in boiling water for 5 minutes to remove impurities. Drain and rinse.
  2. Char the onion and ginger over an open flame or in a hot pan until slightly blackened.
  3. Place bones and brisket in a large pot, add about 5 liters of water, and bring to a simmer. Skim off foam as needed.
  4. Add charred onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon, cloves, and coriander seeds. Simmer gently for 4–6 hours.
  5. Remove brisket after 1.5–2 hours, let it cool slightly, and slice thinly. Continue simmering the broth.
  6. Season the broth with fish sauce, salt, and rock sugar to taste. Strain the broth before serving.
  7. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  8. To serve, place noodles in a bowl, top with brisket slices and raw beef, then ladle in the hot broth to cook the raw beef. Garnish with scallions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
